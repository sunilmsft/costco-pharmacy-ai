Elevating the Pharmacy Member Experience

How an intelligent assistant layer can help members get more value from the services Costco Pharmacy already offers — faster, easier, and more personally.

The Opportunity

From “navigate and find” to “ask and done.”

Today, members need to know what they’re looking for and where to find it. The opportunity isn’t to fix what’s broken — it’s to meet members where they are and help them accomplish what they came to do, conversationally.

Today’s Experience

  • 1 Member arrives at rx.costco.com
  • 2 Scans navigation for the right link
  • 3 Clicks through to a specific service page
  • 4 Reads content to understand options
  • 5 Navigates to another page to take action
  • 6 May call the pharmacy if unsure

With an Intelligent Assistant

  • 1 Member arrives or opens the assistant
  • 2 Sees personalized prompts based on their history
  • 3 States what they need in their own words
  • 4 Assistant guides them through it, step by step
  • 5 Action completed — confirmation and next steps
What It Enables

Six capabilities that compound member value.

Personalized Greeting & Smart Prompts

Recognize returning members and surface their most likely next action — refill due, prescription ready, upcoming immunization window.

“Welcome back, Sarah. Your Lisinopril refill is available. Want to refill now?”

Conversational Task Completion

Members accomplish tasks by describing what they need rather than navigating menus. Transfer, check coverage, schedule — all in the conversation.

“I need to move my prescription from Walgreens” → walks through the transfer in 3 steps

Guided Service Discovery

Proactively connect members with services they qualify for but may not know about — FSA/HSA acceptance, pet medications, partner services.

“Did you know your HSA card is now accepted at every Costco register?”

Fulfillment Recommendation

With 4 delivery options, help members choose the right one for their situation — urgency, location, preference.

“Need it today? Same-day delivery is available. Or Pay Ahead and skip the line.”

Cross-Service Intelligence

Connect related needs that span multiple service areas. Travel planning that links immunizations + medication refills.

“Traveling to Brazil? You’ll want Hep A and Typhoid, plus let’s refill your meds.”

Conversation Continuity

Email a summary of what was discussed, next steps, and relevant links. Pick up where you left off next time.

“I’ve emailed you a summary. Your transfer will be ready in 48 hours.”
Why It Matters

Business impact for the pharmacy and the member.

Each capability maps directly to measurable outcomes that matter to the business and the member experience.

Reduce Inbound Call Volume

The most common pharmacy calls — refill status, transfer process, insurance questions — are exactly what conversational AI handles best.

Industry benchmark: 25-40% call deflection in Year 1

Increase Service Adoption

Members don’t use services they don’t discover. Proactive recommendations drive adoption of Pay Ahead, Mail Order, and Sesame.

Surfaces services at the moment of relevance

Deepen Member Loyalty

A pharmacy that remembers you, anticipates your needs, and makes complex tasks simple becomes part of why you keep your membership.

Pharmacy is a top-3 reason for Costco membership renewal

Competitive Differentiation

CVS, Walgreens, and Amazon Pharmacy are investing heavily in digital. Costco’s advantage — value + trust — an intelligent assistant extends both.

First-mover advantage in pharmacy-specific conversational AI
How This Could Unfold

A phased approach that delivers value early.

This isn’t a multi-year platform rebuild. It’s an experience layer that sits on top of existing systems and can launch incrementally.

1

Informational Assistant

Answer common questions, guide navigation, surface relevant services. No system integration required — just existing content made conversational. Immediate call deflection value.

2

Personalized & Action-Oriented

Recognize logged-in members, show prescription status, enable refills and scheduling within the conversation. Requires pharmacy system integration.

3

Proactive & Predictive

Anticipate member needs — refill reminders, vaccine eligibility, coverage changes. The assistant becomes a trusted health companion.

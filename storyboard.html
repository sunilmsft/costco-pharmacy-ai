Member Perspective

Pharmacy journeys are different.

⏱️
"I need this medication today."
Urgency — needs the fastest fulfillment path
👨‍👩‍👧
"I'm managing prescriptions for my family."
Complexity — multiple people, multiple medications
❓
"I'm not sure if I need an appointment."
Uncertainty — doesn't know the right next step
🏥
"I'm transferring from another pharmacy."
New to Costco — needs clear process guidance
Members may arrive with urgency, uncertainty, or caregiving responsibility. The experience should feel clear, calm, and supportive.
The Shift

From navigation to guidance.

Today

Member uses pages, links, and flows to find the right path
  • Find the right page for their need
  • Read through content to understand options
  • Decide which option applies to them
  • Switch between different flows
  • Call when unsure what to do next

With a Guided Experience Layer

Member is supported at each step
  • Ask in natural language what they need
  • Receive plain-language guidance
  • See relevant next steps surfaced clearly
  • Continue one connected journey
  • Escalate to pharmacy staff when human judgment or support is needed
What It Enables

A guide for common member moments.

💊 Refill
"Can I refill this now?"
🔄 Transfer
"Move my prescription from another pharmacy."
💉 Immunization
"Help me find the right appointment path."
🚗 Fulfillment
"What is the best pickup or delivery option?"
👨‍👩‍👧 Family & Pet
"Help me understand what services are available."
The assistant helps members move from intent to action — without needing to understand the site structure first.
Guardrails

Designed for trust from day one.

✓ Guide, not a pharmacist replacement
✓ No diagnosis or independent clinical advice
✓ Approved content and business rules only
✓ Authentication before personal details
✓ Privacy, consent, and auditability built in
✓ Human handoff when needed
✓ Complements existing pharmacy workflows — does not replace them

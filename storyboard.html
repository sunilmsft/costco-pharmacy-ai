The Opportunity

From "navigate and find" to "ask and done."

Today, members need to know what they're looking for and where to find it. The opportunity isn't to fix what's broken — it's to meet members where they are and help them accomplish what they came to do, conversationally.

Today's Experience

  • 1 Member arrives at rx.costco.com
  • 2 Scans navigation for the right link
  • 3 Clicks through to a specific service page
  • 4 Reads content to understand options
  • 5 Navigates to another page to take action
  • 6 May call the pharmacy if unsure

With an Intelligent Assistant

  • 1 Member arrives or opens the assistant
  • 2 Sees personalized prompts based on their history
  • 3 States what they need in their own words
  • 4 Assistant guides them through it, step by step
  • 5 Action completed — confirmation and next steps
What It Enables

Six capabilities that compound member value.

Each of these works independently but becomes significantly more powerful together — creating an experience that feels less like a website and more like a conversation with a knowledgeable pharmacist.

Personalized Greeting & Smart Prompts

Recognize returning members and surface their most likely next action — refill due, prescription ready for pickup, upcoming immunization window.

"Welcome back, Sarah. Your Lisinopril refill is available. Want to refill now?"

Conversational Task Completion

Members accomplish tasks by describing what they need rather than navigating menus. Transfer a prescription, check coverage, schedule a vaccine — all within the conversation.

"I need to move my prescription from Walgreens" → walks through the transfer in 3 steps

Guided Service Discovery

Proactively connect members with services they qualify for but may not know about — FSA/HSA card acceptance, member pricing, fertility partnerships, pet medications.

"Did you know your HSA card is now accepted at every Costco register, including pharmacy?"

Fulfillment Recommendation

With 4 delivery options, help members choose the right one for their situation — urgency, location, preference — rather than requiring them to evaluate each option.

"Need it today? Same-day delivery is available to your area. Or Pay Ahead and pick up at the counter in 15 min."

Cross-Service Intelligence

Connect related needs that span multiple service areas. Travel planning that links immunizations + medication refills. Seasonal wellness that links flu shots + Rx management.

"Traveling to Brazil? You'll want Hep A and Typhoid vaccines, plus let's make sure your medications are refilled before you go."

Conversation Continuity

Email a summary of what was discussed, next steps, and relevant links. Pick up where you left off next time. Never lose the thread.

"I've emailed you a summary. Your transfer will be ready in 48 hours — I'll remind you."
Why It Matters

Business impact for the pharmacy and the member.

This isn't technology for technology's sake. Each capability maps directly to measurable outcomes that matter to the business and the member experience.

Reduce Inbound Call Volume

The most common pharmacy calls — refill status, transfer process, insurance questions — are exactly what conversational AI handles best.

Industry benchmark: 25-40% call deflection in Year 1

Increase Service Adoption

Members don't use services they don't discover. Proactive, contextual recommendations drive adoption of Pay Ahead, Mail Order, and partner services like Sesame.

Surfaces services at the moment of relevance, not just on a page

Deepen Member Loyalty

A pharmacy that remembers you, anticipates your needs, and makes complex tasks simple becomes part of why you keep your membership.

Pharmacy is a top-3 reason for Costco membership renewal

Competitive Differentiation

CVS, Walgreens, and Amazon Pharmacy are investing heavily in digital. Costco's advantage has always been value + trust — an intelligent assistant extends both into digital.

First-mover advantage in pharmacy-specific conversational AI
How This Could Unfold

A phased approach that delivers value early.

This isn't a multi-year platform rebuild. It's an experience layer that sits on top of existing systems and can launch incrementally, proving value at each stage.

1

Informational Assistant

Answer common questions, guide navigation, surface relevant services. No system integration required — just the pharmacy's existing content made conversational. Immediate call deflection value.

2

Personalized & Action-Oriented

Recognize logged-in members, show prescription status, enable refills and scheduling within the conversation. Requires integration with pharmacy systems but dramatically increases utility.

3

Proactive & Predictive

Anticipate member needs — refill reminders, vaccine eligibility windows, plan coverage changes. The assistant becomes a trusted health companion, not just a tool you visit when something's needed.
